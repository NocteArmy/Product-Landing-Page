Roland TD-11K V-Drums

COMPACT AND AFFORDABLE V-DRUMS, POWERED BY SUPERNATURAL SOUND

Enhanced the SuperNATURAL technology, the TD-11 V-Drums V-Compact Series brings a more natural and realistic playing experience to drummers of any skill level and budget. Affordable and easy to use, these new electronic kits are perfect for a variety of applications, including practice, teaching, recording, live performance, and more

Features

  • SuperNATURAL-enhanced sound engine with Behaviour Modeling provides an expressive and natural drumming experience
  • Diverse sound library covers a wide variety of musical genres for performance and practice
  • Professionality recorded backings songs onboard, ideal for play-along practice and performance
  • Practice features include Quick Record/Quick Play, plus a unique COACH function that helps players of all levels build drumming skills effectively
